HOW TO PLAY LIU BEI ESCAPE

HOW TO PLAY!

Guide your character through challenges, dodging obstacles and enemies to reach the finish line. Collect two keys to unlock the next level and carefully manage your hearts. Remember, collecting coins and finishing quickly will boost your score. Good luck!

  • Coins

    Collect coins to increase your score.

    •

  • Key

    Collect 2 keys and reach the finish line to pass the level.

    •

  • Rune

    If you collect a Rune, it will increase your hearts by 1.

    •

You can attack to change the direction of enemies or arrows.

Avoid obstacles and enemies, collect 2 keys, and reach the flag to pass the level.

HOW TO CONTROL!

W - Jump

A - Move Left

D - Move Right

F - Attack

SPACE - Respawn or Proceed to the Next Level

I - Zoom In

O - Zoom out
